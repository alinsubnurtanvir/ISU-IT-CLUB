About IT Club

Who We Are

The ISU IT Club is a community of students passionate about technology. We provide a platform for students to enhance their technical skills, work on projects, and connect with industry professionals.

Our mission is to foster innovation and technological advancement within the university community and prepare members for successful careers in the tech industry.

What We Do

  • Weekly coding workshops and hackathons
  • Tech talks from industry experts
  • Collaborative projects to build practical experience
  • Networking events with tech companies
